Please read this policy carefully. By creating an account or using the Service, you acknowledge the practices described here.

## 1. Our role

TrueHR is a human-resource management service that is made available to employees, contractors, and staff of organizations ("**Employers**") that subscribe to it. In most cases your Employer decides what information is collected about you and why, and instructs us to process it on their behalf. For those purposes your Employer is responsible for that information, and we act as a service provider processing it under the Employer's instructions. This policy describes our own practices; your Employer's internal HR policies may also apply.

## 2. Information we collect

**a. Account and profile information**
Name, employee code, official and personal email address, phone number, date of birth, gender, designation, department, grade, date of joining, reporting/functional managers, work location, and residential/permanent address.

**b. Financial and identity information**
Bank account number, IFSC and bank details, PAN, and Aadhaar number, where provided as part of onboarding or payroll. This category is sensitive and is stored in encrypted form (see Section 6).

**c. Location information**
- **Attendance and on-duty:** your device location at the moment you mark attendance or apply for on-duty/field work.
- **Tour tracking:** when you start a tour, the app records your location continuously — including while the app is in the background or the screen is locked — until you end the tour, so that your route and distance can be calculated. A persistent notification is shown while tracking is active. Location tracking runs only during an active tour and stops when you end it.
- **Geo-tags:** the location attached to photos you capture using the Geo-Tag feature.

**d. Camera and photos**
Photos you capture for attendance, on-duty, geo-tagging, or supporting documents (for example a leave certificate). Geo-tagged photos may have your name, employee code, date/time, address, and coordinates overlaid on the image.

**e. HR activity data**
Attendance and working-hours records, leave and comp-off requests, tasks assigned to or by you, support tickets, tour and geo-tag records, payroll/salary-slip data, and resignation requests.

**f. Technical and usage information**
Authentication tokens, app and error logs, and basic device information necessary to operate and secure the Service.

## 3. How we use information

We use the information to:
- create and manage your account and authenticate you;
- record and display attendance, working hours, leave, comp-off, and tasks;
- calculate and provide tours (route and distance) and geo-tagged records;
- generate and deliver salary slips and payroll information;
- process onboarding, resignation, and support requests;
- provide the Service to your Employer and enable your managers/HR to perform their duties;
- maintain security, prevent misuse, and keep audit records of key actions;
- operate, troubleshoot, and improve the Service.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising.

## 4. Location and background tracking (your control)

Location and background-location access are used only for the attendance, on-duty, geo-tag, and tour features described above. You control these through your device's permission settings and can withdraw them at any time. If you disable location or background access, location-dependent features (such as tour tracking) will not function correctly. Tour tracking only records while a tour is active and stops when you end the tour.

## 5. How we share information

We share information only as needed to operate the Service:
- **With your Employer** — your HR administrators and, where applicable, your managers can view the records relevant to their role (for example attendance, leave approvals, team tasks, tours, and resignations).
- **With service providers** — hosting and infrastructure providers who process data on our behalf to run the Service, under confidentiality obligations.
- **For legal reasons** — where required to comply with applicable law, a lawful request from authorities, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users, the public, or the Service.
- **On business transfer** — if the business is merged, acquired, or reorganized, information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

## 6. Data security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, including:
- encryption of sensitive fields (such as bank account, PAN, and Aadhaar) at rest;
- encrypted transport (HTTPS/TLS) for data in transit;
- role-based access controls so users see only what their role permits;
- authentication tokens with expiry, and audit logging of key actions.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Data retention

We retain information for as long as your account is active and as long as needed to provide the Service to your Employer, and thereafter as required for legitimate business, legal, tax, or record-keeping purposes. Your Employer may instruct us to retain, export, or delete records associated with your account.

## 8. Your choices

- **Access and correction:** you can view much of your information within the app. For changes to official records, contact your HR administrator.
- **Device permissions:** you can grant or revoke location, background location, camera, and notification permissions in your device settings.
- **Account:** accounts are provisioned and deactivated by your Employer's HR/administrators.

Requests relating to your information should generally be directed to your Employer, who instructs us on how your data is handled.

## 9. Children

The Service is intended for use by working professionals and is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## 10. Changes to this policy
